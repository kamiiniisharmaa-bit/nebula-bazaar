🔭 Privacy Policy · Your Data, Our Promise

At NEBULA BAZAAR, your privacy orbits at the center of everything we do. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information across our cosmic marketplace.

Effective: June 10, 2026 | Last reviewed: June 10, 2026

1. What Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services, process orders, and create a personalized shopping experience. The types of information include:

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Identity DataFull name, username, date of birthAccount creation & order verification
Contact DataEmail address, shipping address, phone numberDelivery, order updates & customer support
Transaction DataPayment details, purchase history, wishlist itemsProcess payments & recommendations
Technical DataIP address, browser type, device identifiersSite security & analytics
Usage DataPages viewed, time spent, click patternsImprove user experience & marketing

We do not collect sensitive personal data (like biometric or health information) unless you explicitly provide it for a specific purpose (e.g., accessibility requests).

2. How We Gather Your Information

  • Direct interactions: When you create an account, place an order, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact support.
  • Automated technologies: Cookies, beacons, and similar tracking tech as you browse our site.
  • Third-party sources: Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) for transaction verification, and shipping carriers for delivery updates.
Cookie Nebula: We use essential cookies for site functionality and optional analytics cookies. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Personal Data

We process your information under legitimate business interests, contract fulfillment, or with your consent. Common uses include:

  • Process and deliver orders, including payments, returns, and exchanges.
  • Manage your account and provide customer support.
  • Send you transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates).
  • Improve our website, products, and personalize recommendations.
  • Send marketing communications (only if you opt-in; you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • Detect fraud, security incidents, and comply with legal obligations.

4. When We Share Your Information

We never sell your personal data to third parties. However, we share data with trusted partners to operate our business:

  • Service providers: Payment gateways, shipping carriers, email platforms, and analytics (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Legal compliance: If required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred with notice.

All third-party partners are contractually obligated to respect your data and use it only for specified services.

5. Security & Retention: Your Cosmic Shield

We implement industry-standard security measures (SSL encryption, firewalls, access controls) to protect your data against unauthorized access. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy — typically 5 years after your last interaction, or as required by tax/legal obligations.

Your responsibility: Always keep your account credentials confidential. If you suspect any breach, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights Over Your Data

Depending on your location (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your data (subject to legal exceptions).
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails (link in every email).
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@nebulabazaar.com. We will respond within 30 days. We will never discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

7. Children's Data (Under 16)

NEBULA BAZAAR does not knowingly sell products to children under the age of 16, nor do we intentionally collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete it promptly.

8. Interstellar Data Transfers

Our servers and operations are based in the United States. If you access our site from outside the US, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US, where data protection laws may differ. By using our services, you consent to this transfer. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place.

9. External Links & Social Features

Our website may contain links to partner brands, social media platforms, or user-generated content. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party websites. We encourage you to read their policies before interacting.

10. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in practices or legal requirements. The "Effective Date" at the top indicates the latest revision. Material changes will be notified via email or a prominent notice on our website. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
